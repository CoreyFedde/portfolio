This is an about

I’m a web developer currently enrolled in the General Assembly Web Development Immersive. I arrived to the field with a pretty unique background working first as a journalist with a focus on science and breaking news and later in business strategy consulting as an assistant in a firm focused on technology companies. The takeaway from both experiences was that there is a need for solution-driven products and people that can make them – newspaper readers want to read about solutions and most problems facing companies I worked with originated from building products that didn’t solve a problem for their customers. I’d like to take that lesson and apply it to development. My goal is to be a solution-driven programmer and create web applications that help solve tangible problems. After the immersive, I'll be looking for a team with a similar mindset and projects where I can refine my craft while contributing meaningfully.
